Tiger
The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest species among the Felidae and classified in the genus Panthera. It is most recognizable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a lighter underside. It is an apex predator, primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and bovids.
Coleus
Coleus is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lamiaceae, native to tropical and subtropical regions. They are known for their vibrant foliage, which comes in a variety of colors and patterns. Coleus plants are often used as ornamental plants in gardens and landscapes.
Plumeria
Plumeria, commonly known as frangipani, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae. They are native to tropical and subtropical regions and are known for their beautiful, fragrant flowers. Plumeria flowers are often used in leis and other floral arrangements.
Monster buggy

Desert Rose
The Desert Rose (Adenium obesum) is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula and parts of Africa. It is known for its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers and thick, swollen stems that store water, making it well-adapted to arid environments.
